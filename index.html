About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at Tsinghua University focusing on Fault-tolerant Quantum Computing and qLDPC Decoding Architectures. My recent work includes syndrome extraction circuit compilation, scalable decoding architectures for quantum error correction and AI for quantum computing.
